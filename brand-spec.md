# Portfolio design system

- Direction: editorial product casebook with an Issuu-like double-page reader, book spine, edge controls and 3D page turns.
- Narrative: recruiter scans evidence in 3-5 minutes; each spread answers one product question.
- Palette: paper `#F3F0E8`, ink `#171A1D`, teal `#0E7C78`, blue `#154C79`, line `#C9C5BA`.
- Typography: Songti SC/STSong for display; PingFang SC/Microsoft YaHei for body; Avenir Next for numeric labels.
- Spacing: 8px base grid; page padding 32-58px fluid.
- Radius: square paper and diagrams; no decorative card rounding.
- Shadow: one book-level shadow only.
- Motion: 720ms spine-based 3D page turn with `cubic-bezier(.2,.68,.15,1)`; reduced-motion toggle included.
- Assets: portrait and resume are user-provided; Youxian prototype/flow/structure images are extracted from the user-provided project document. No brand logo is required because this is a personal casebook.
